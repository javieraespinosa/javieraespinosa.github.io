    About

    I am a computer scientist specialized in the domains of databases & distributed systems. My core experience concerns cloud & service based Big Data management & processing.

    I work as postdoctoral researcher at University of Lyon. I am also researcher at the ERIC research lab, and associate researcher at the French-Mexican LAFMIA lab. I am also former researcher of the Barcelona Supercomputing Centre, TU Delft and the Grenoble Informatics Laboratory.

    I am fellow of the Mexican AMEXCOMP and SNI (level C), and member of the ACM.
