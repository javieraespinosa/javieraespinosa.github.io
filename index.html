    About

    I am a computer scientist specialized in the domains of databases & distributed systems. My core experience concerns cloud & service based Big Data management & processing.

    I work as postdoctoral researcher at University of Lyon 3 and the Laboratoy of Informatics of Grenoble ( HADAS group). I am also researcher of the LAFMIA French-Mexican laboratory, and former researcher of the Barcelona Supercomputing Centre and TU Delft.

    I am fellow of the Mexican AMEXCOMP and SNI (level C), and member of the ACM.
