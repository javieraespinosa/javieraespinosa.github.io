Javier Espinosa

Associate Professor

CPE Lyon

University of Lyon

I am a computer scientist specialized in the domains of databases & distributed systems. My core experience concerns cloud & service based Big Data management & processing.

I work as Assistant Professor of Computer Science in the CPE Engineering School of the University of Lyon. I am a former researcher of the Barcelona Supercomputing Centre, TU Delft, the Grenoble Informatics Laboratory and the French-Mexican LAFMIA lab.

I am fellow of the Mexican AMEXCOMP and SNI (level C), and member of the ACM.
